The DEAP dataset is a multimodal dataset for the analysis of human affective states. It includes EEG and peripheral physiological signals such as ECG, GSR, and EMG, recorded from 32 participants while watching music videos.

http://www.eecs.qmul.ac.uk/mmv/datasets/deap/

The BNCI Horizon 2020 project provides several datasets containing EEG and ECG data for various applications, including motor imagery, P300, and steady-state visually evoked potentials (SSVEP) experiments.
http://bnci-horizon-2020.eu/database/data-sets 


The DREAMS Sleep Database is a collection of sleep-related datasets, some of which contain joint EEG and ECG recordings. For example, the DREAMS Subjects Database includes EEG, EOG, EMG, and ECG data from 20 healthy subjects during sleep.
http://www.tcts.fpms.ac.be/~devuyst/Databases/DatabaseSleep/

The CHB-MIT Scalp EEG Database contains scalp EEG recordings from pediatric subjects with intractable seizures. It includes 24-hour continuous recordings for each subject, with ECG data available in some cases. https://physionet.org/content/chbmit/1.0.0/

The Bonn University EEG Database consists of EEG recordings from five different groups, including healthy subjects and patients with epilepsy. It provides simultaneous ECG data for some subjects.
http://epileptologie-bonn.de/cms/front_content.php?idcat=193&lang=3
